# Interview Introduction - Presentacion en entrevistas

## Introduccion

La primera pregunta en casi toda entrevista tecnica es **"Tell me about yourself"**. Aunque parece simple, es tu oportunidad de causar una excelente primera impresion. En esta leccion aprenderas a estructurar una presentacion profesional en ingles que destaque tu experiencia y entusiasmo.

---

## La pregunta: "Tell me about yourself"

Esta pregunta NO significa "cuentame tu vida." El entrevistador quiere saber:

1. **Quien eres profesionalmente** (rol actual)
2. **Que experiencia tienes** (trayectoria relevante)
3. **Por que estas aqui** (que te motiva / por que esta empresa)

---

## La formula del Elevator Pitch

Estructura tu respuesta en 3 partes, de 60 a 90 segundos:

```
Parte 1: PRESENTE - Tu rol actual
"I'm a [role] with [X] years of experience in [technologies]."
"Currently, I'm working on/at [company/project]."

Parte 2: PASADO - Experiencia relevante
"Previously, I worked at [company] where I [key achievement]."
"I've built/shipped/led [notable project or result]."

Parte 3: FUTURO - Por que esta oportunidad
"I'm excited about [something specific about the company/role]."
"I'm looking to [grow in area / contribute to mission]."
```

---

## Ejemplos completos por nivel

### Junior Developer

```
"I'm a frontend developer with about a year of experience 
working primarily with React and TypeScript. I recently 
graduated from [university/bootcamp] where I built a 
full-stack e-commerce application as my capstone project.

Currently, I'm working on a personal project — a task 
management app using Next.js and Supabase — where I'm 
learning about server-side rendering and authentication.

I'm really excited about this role because your team works 
on developer tools, and I'm passionate about building 
products that help other developers be more productive."
```

### Mid-Level Developer

```
"I'm a full-stack developer with three years of experience 
specializing in Flutter and Node.js. Currently, I'm working 
at [Company] where I lead the mobile team building our 
consumer-facing application, which has over 50,000 active users.

Previously, I was at a startup where I built the entire 
backend API from scratch, handling payments integration and 
real-time notifications. I also introduced CI/CD pipelines 
that reduced our deployment time by 60%.

I'm interested in this position because I want to work at 
a larger scale and contribute to a product that millions of 
people use. Your engineering blog posts about microservices 
migration really resonated with my experience."
```

### Senior Developer

```
"I'm a senior software engineer with seven years of experience 
in backend systems and distributed architecture. Currently, 
I'm at [Company] where I architect and maintain services 
handling two million requests per day.

Over the past few years, I've led the migration from a 
monolithic system to microservices, mentored a team of five 
engineers, and drove the adoption of Kubernetes across the 
organization. I also contributed to our open-source monitoring 
toolkit which has over 3,000 GitHub stars.

I'm drawn to this role because your team is tackling 
interesting challenges in real-time data processing, and 
I believe my experience with event-driven systems would 
be a strong fit."
```

## Vocabulario clave para presentaciones

| Ingles | Espanol | Ejemplo |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **I specialize in** | Me especializo en | *"I specialize in mobile development."* |
| **I have experience with** | Tengo experiencia con | *"I have experience with cloud infrastructure."* |
| **I'm proficient in** | Soy competente en | *"I'm proficient in Python and Go."* |
| **I'm passionate about** | Me apasiona | *"I'm passionate about clean architecture."* |
| **I'm excited about** | Me entusiasma | *"I'm excited about this opportunity."* |
| **I led / I spearheaded** | Lidere / Encabece | *"I led the backend rewrite."* |
| **I contributed to** | Contribui a | *"I contributed to several open-source projects."* |
| **I'm looking to** | Busco / Quiero | *"I'm looking to grow into a tech lead role."* |
| **I'm drawn to** | Me atrae | *"I'm drawn to your company's mission."* |
| **I bring** | Aporto | *"I bring strong experience in scalable systems."* |

```
"What drew me to your company is..."
"Looking ahead, I want to..."
"That's what brings me here today — ..."
```

## Errores comunes a evitar

| Error | Por que es malo | Mejor alternativa |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| Hablar mas de 2 minutos | Pierdes la atencion | Maximo 90 segundos |
| Empezar con "Well, I was born in..." | No es relevante | Empieza con tu rol actual |
| Listar todas tus tecnologias | Suena como un CV leido | Menciona 2-3 relevantes al puesto |
| "I'm just a junior" | Te minimizas | "I'm an eager developer with fresh skills in..." |
| No mencionar la empresa | Parece respuesta generica | Investiga y menciona algo especifico |
| Hablar solo de tecnologias | Eres mas que una lista de skills | Incluye logros y motivaciones |

## Otras preguntas de apertura

| Pregunta | Que buscan | Como responder |
|----------|-----------|----------------|
| **"Walk me through your resume"** | Version cronologica de tu carrera | Orden cronologico, enfocate en lo relevante |
| **"Why are you looking for a new role?"** | Tu motivacion | Enfocate en lo positivo: crecimiento, nuevos retos |
| **"What do you know about us?"** | Si investigaste la empresa | Menciona producto, mision, tecnologias |
| **"Why this company?"** | Tu interes genuino | Conecta tu experiencia con su mision |

```
// "Why are you looking for a new role?"
BUENO: "I've learned a lot at my current company, and now 
I'm looking for opportunities to work at a larger scale 
and tackle more complex technical challenges."

MALO: "My current job is boring and my boss is terrible."

// "What do you know about us?"
BUENO: "I know you're building a developer platform that 
simplifies cloud deployments. I read your recent blog post 
about edge computing, and I found your approach really 
innovative."

MALO: "Uh... you're a tech company, right?"
```

## Practica: construye tu elevator pitch

Completa esta plantilla con tu informacion real:

```
"I'm a _____________ with _____ years of experience in 
_____________ and _____________. Currently, I'm working 
on/at _____________ where I _____________.

Previously, I _____________ where I _____________. 
One of my key achievements was _____________.

I'm excited about this role because _____________. 
I believe my experience in _____________ would be 
a strong fit for your team."
```

---

## Consejo final

> Practica tu presentacion en voz alta hasta que suene natural, no memorizada. Grabate y escuchate. Ajusta segun el puesto: si aplicas para un rol de frontend, enfatiza tu experiencia en frontend; si es backend, enfatiza backend. La misma persona puede tener 5 versiones diferentes de su "tell me about yourself."
