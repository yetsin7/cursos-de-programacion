# Lesson 04: Writing a Tech Cover Letter

## Introduccion

La cover letter (carta de presentacion) es tu oportunidad de mostrar personalidad y motivacion mas alla del resume. No todas las empresas la piden, pero cuando la piden, una buena cover letter puede diferenciarte de cientos de candidatos con skills similares.

---

## Estructura de la Cover Letter

| Seccion | Longitud | Contenido |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Opening** | 2-3 oraciones | Hook + por que aplicas |
| **Body 1** | 3-4 oraciones | Experiencia relevante |
| **Body 2** | 3-4 oraciones | Por que esta empresa |
| **Closing** | 2-3 oraciones | Call to action |

**Longitud total:** 250-400 palabras (3-4 parrafos cortos).

---

## Opening Paragraph (El Hook)

### Formulas efectivas

| Formula | Ejemplo |
|---------|---------|
| **Interes directo** | "I'm writing to express my interest in the Senior Flutter Developer position at [Company]." |
| **Conexion personal** | "As a long-time user of [Product], I was excited to see the opening for a Mobile Developer." |
| **Referencia** | "My colleague [Name] suggested I apply for the Backend Engineer role on your team." |
| **Logro relevante** | "Having shipped 3 mobile apps with 100K+ combined downloads, I'm excited to apply for..." |

### Ejemplos de opening

```
I'm writing to express my interest in the Senior Mobile 
Developer position at TechCorp. With 5 years of experience 
building Flutter apps for production environments, I'm 
confident I can contribute to your mobile team's goals.
```

```
As someone who has used [Product] daily for the past two 
years, I was thrilled to see the opening for a Frontend 
Engineer. I'd love the opportunity to contribute to a 
product I'm genuinely passionate about.
```

### Openings que debes evitar

| Malo | Por que |
|------|---------|
| "To Whom It May Concern" | Muy impersonal. Usa "Dear Hiring Team" |
| "I am writing to apply for a job" | Demasiado generico |
| "I need a job and your company looks good" | Suena desesperado |
| "My name is John and I am a developer" | Obvio, ya lo dice el resume |

## Body Paragraph 1: Tu experiencia relevante

Conecta tu experiencia directamente con lo que busca la empresa.

### Formula

```
In my current role at [Company], I [achievement relevant to job].
[Specific example with metrics]. This experience has prepared 
me to [what you'll do at new company].
```

### Ejemplo

```
In my current role at StartupXYZ, I developed and maintained 
a cross-platform mobile app serving 50,000+ daily active users. 
I architected the offline-first data layer and implemented a 
CI/CD pipeline that reduced our release cycle from weekly to 
daily. This hands-on experience with Flutter at scale has 
prepared me to tackle the challenges outlined in your job 
description.
```

### Frases utiles para esta seccion

| Frase | Uso |
|-------|-----|
| "In my current role at..." | Introducir tu experiencia actual |
| "Over the past X years, I've..." | Resumir tu trayectoria |
| "I have a proven track record of..." | Destacar resultados |
| "Most recently, I..." | Logro mas reciente |
| "One of my key achievements was..." | Destacar un logro especifico |
| "This experience has given me..." | Conectar experiencia con el puesto |

---

## Body Paragraph 2: Por que esta empresa

Los reclutadores quieren saber que investigaste la empresa. Esta seccion es donde demuestras que no envias la misma carta a todas las empresas.

### Ejemplo

```
I'm particularly excited about TechCorp because of your 
commitment to building accessible technology for emerging 
markets. Your recent blog post on offline-first architecture 
resonated with my own experience building apps for users with 
limited connectivity. I'm also drawn to your engineering 
culture of code reviews and knowledge sharing, which aligns 
with how I believe the best software is built.
```

### Frases utiles

| Frase | Uso |
|-------|-----|
| "I'm particularly excited about [Company] because..." | Motivacion |
| "Your mission to [mission] resonates with me because..." | Conexion con la mision |
| "I admire your team's approach to..." | Elogiar algo especifico |
| "I've been following your work on..." | Mostrar conocimiento |
| "What draws me to [Company] is..." | Afinidad |
| "Your engineering blog on [topic] inspired me to..." | Investigacion especifica |

## Closing Paragraph

### Formula

```
[Express enthusiasm]. [Summarize value]. [Call to action].
```

### Ejemplo

```
I'm excited about the possibility of joining TechCorp and 
contributing to your mobile team. I believe my experience 
with Flutter, clean architecture, and performance optimization 
would be a strong addition to your engineering efforts. I 
look forward to discussing how I can contribute to your 
team's success.
```

### Frases de cierre

| Frase | Uso |
|-------|-----|
| "I look forward to discussing..." | Expresar interes en hablar |
| "I'd welcome the opportunity to..." | Tono formal y profesional |
| "Thank you for considering my application." | Agradecimiento |
| "I'm happy to provide any additional information." | Disponibilidad |
| "I'm available for an interview at your convenience." | Flexibilidad |

## Cover Letter completa de ejemplo

```
Dear Hiring Team,

I'm writing to express my interest in the Senior Mobile 
Developer position at TechCorp. With 5 years of experience 
building production Flutter applications and a passion for 
clean, maintainable code, I'm excited about the opportunity 
to contribute to your mobile team.

In my current role at StartupXYZ, I lead the development of 
a cross-platform e-commerce app serving 50,000+ daily active 
users. I architected the app's offline-first data layer, 
implemented comprehensive testing that brought coverage to 
90%, and set up a CI/CD pipeline that reduced our release 
cycle from biweekly to daily. I also mentored three junior 
developers, fostering a culture of code reviews and knowledge 
sharing.

I'm particularly drawn to TechCorp's mission of making 
technology accessible in emerging markets. Your recent open-
source contributions to the Flutter ecosystem — especially 
the connectivity_plus package — demonstrate a commitment to 
the community that I deeply respect. I'd love to bring my 
experience with offline architectures and performance 
optimization to support these efforts.

I look forward to discussing how my skills and experience 
align with your team's goals. Thank you for considering my 
application.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## Errores comunes

| Error | Correccion |
|-------|-----------|
| Copiar el resume en la cover letter | La cover letter cuenta una historia, no repite datos |
| Escribir mas de una pagina | Mantener 250-400 palabras |
| Hablar solo de ti | Conecta tus skills con las necesidades de la empresa |
| No investigar la empresa | Menciona algo especifico de la empresa |
| Usar la misma carta para todas las aplicaciones | Personaliza cada una |
| Errores gramaticales | Revisa con Grammarly o similar |

## Vocabulario esencial

| Termino | Significado |
|---------|-------------|
| **Cover letter** | Carta de presentacion |
| **Hiring team** | Equipo de contratacion |
| **Position** | Puesto / Posicion |
| **Opportunity** | Oportunidad |
| **Application** | Solicitud / Aplicacion |
| **Compensation** | Compensacion (salario + beneficios) |
| **Relocation** | Reubicacion |
| **Notice period** | Periodo de preaviso |
| **Availability** | Disponibilidad |
| **References** | Referencias |

---

## Resumen

- La cover letter tiene 4 secciones: hook, experiencia, por que la empresa, cierre
- Personaliza cada cover letter para la empresa especifica
- Conecta tu experiencia con las necesidades del puesto
- Demuestra que investigaste la empresa mencionando algo especifico
- Mantenla entre 250-400 palabras (3-4 parrafos)
- Usa un tono profesional pero con personalidad
