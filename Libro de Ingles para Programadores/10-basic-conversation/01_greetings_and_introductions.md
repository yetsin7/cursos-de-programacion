# Greetings and Introductions

## Saludos e introducciones para programadores

Las primeras impresiones cuentan, especialmente en entrevistas, onboarding y cuando te unes a un nuevo equipo. Aquí aprenderás cómo saludar y presentarte en inglés en ambientes tech.

---

## 1. Saludos básicos

### Formales (entrevistas, clientes, emails)

| Saludo | Contexto |
|--------|----------|
| Hello. | Universal, siempre correcto. |
| Good morning. | Antes del mediodía. |
| Good afternoon. | Después del mediodía. |
| Good evening. | Al atardecer/noche. |

### Informales (equipo, Slack, standups)

| Saludo | Contexto |
|--------|----------|
| Hi! | El más común en tech. |
| Hey! | Casual, entre compañeros. |
| Hey there! | Amistoso y casual. |
| What's up? | Muy informal, entre amigos. |
| Morning! | Versión corta de "Good morning". |
| Yo! | Muy informal, solo con confianza. |

> **Consejo:** En entornos tech remotos, **"Hi"** y **"Hey"** son los más usados. No necesitas ser demasiado formal con tu equipo.

---

## 2. Presentarte profesionalmente

### Estructura básica

```text
Hi, I'm [nombre]. I'm a [rol] at [empresa/equipo].
I work with [tecnologías principales].
```

### Ejemplos por rol

```text
Hi, I'm Carlos. I'm a frontend developer.
I mainly work with React and TypeScript.

Hey, I'm Ana. I'm a backend engineer at Acme Corp.
I work with Node.js, PostgreSQL, and AWS.

Hello, I'm David. I'm a full-stack developer.
I build web applications with Next.js and Supabase.

Hi, I'm Maria. I'm a mobile developer.
I build cross-platform apps with Flutter.

Hey, I'm Luis. I'm a DevOps engineer.
I work with Kubernetes, Docker, and CI/CD pipelines.
```

### Información adicional que puedes agregar

```text
I've been working here for about two years.
I joined the team last month.
Before this, I worked at a fintech startup.
I'm based in Managua, Nicaragua.
I mainly focus on the authentication module.
```

## 3. Responder cuando te presentan a alguien

| Frase | Formalidad |
|-------|-----------|
| Nice to meet you. | Formal/estándar |
| Nice to meet you too. | Respuesta al anterior |
| Pleased to meet you. | Más formal |
| Good to meet you. | Semi-formal |
| Great to finally meet you! | Cuando ya se conocían por chat |
| Hey, I've heard great things about you. | Amistoso y positivo |

### Conversación típica de onboarding

```text
A: "Hi, I'm Sarah. I'm the tech lead for the backend team."
B: "Nice to meet you, Sarah. I'm Carlos. I'm the new frontend dev."
A: "Welcome to the team! How are you settling in?"
B: "Thanks! Everything's going well so far. Still getting familiar 
    with the codebase."
A: "Let me know if you need any help. I'm happy to walk you through 
    the architecture."
B: "That would be great, thanks!"
```

## 4. Small talk en ambientes tech

El **small talk** es conversación ligera antes de entrar al tema principal. En tech, se habla de:

### Temas comunes

| Tema | Ejemplo |
|------|---------|
| El clima (clásico) | "How's the weather over there?" |
| El fin de semana | "Did you do anything fun this weekend?" |
| Proyectos personales | "Working on any side projects?" |
| Tecnologías nuevas | "Have you tried the new React Server Components?" |
| Conferencias/eventos | "Did you watch any talks from the last conference?" |
| El café | "How's your coffee today?" (muy común en standups) |

### Ejemplos de small talk

```text
"How's your day going?"
"Pretty good. Just spent the morning debugging a weird CSS issue."

"Working on anything interesting?"
"Yeah, I'm trying out Rust for a side project. It's fun but steep."

"How was your weekend?"
"It was great. I finally finished migrating my blog to Astro."
```

### Frases para continuar la conversación

```text
That's cool!
Oh, interesting! How's that going?
Nice! I've been meaning to try that.
Yeah, I've heard good things about it.
That sounds like a fun project.
```

## 5. Despedidas

### Formales

```text
It was nice meeting you.
Thank you for your time.
Have a good day.
Take care.
```

### Informales (fin del día, fin de reunión)

```text
See you tomorrow!
See you at the standup.
Bye! / Bye-bye!
Have a good one!
Talk to you later.
Later! (muy casual)
Have a good weekend! (los viernes)
Enjoy the rest of your day!
```

### Al final de una reunión

```text
Thanks, everyone.
Great meeting, thanks all.
Alright, talk to you tomorrow.
Sounds good. See you at the retro.
```

---

## 6. Frases para situaciones específicas

### Cuando llegas tarde a una reunión

```text
Sorry I'm late.
Sorry, I had a conflict with another meeting.
Apologies for joining late.
```

### Cuando alguien nuevo se une

```text
Welcome to the team!
Good to have you here.
Let me know if you need anything.
Don't hesitate to ask questions.
```

### Cuando te vas de vacaciones

```text
I'll be out next week.
I'm on PTO starting Monday.
See you in two weeks!
I'll be offline until the 15th.
```

### Cuando alguien regresa

```text
Welcome back!
How was your time off?
Hope you had a good break.
```

## 7. Errores comunes de hispanohablantes

| Incorrecto | Correcto | Explicación |
|------------|----------|-------------|
| I'm developer. | I'm a developer. | Falta el artículo "a" |
| I have 25 years. | I'm 25 years old. | En inglés se usa "to be", no "to have" |
| I am agree. | I agree. | "Agree" es verbo, no adjetivo |
| Nice to meet you. (segunda vez) | Good to see you again. | "Nice to meet you" es solo la primera vez |
| Good night. (como saludo) | Good evening. | "Good night" es despedida, no saludo |
| I am from 5 years in this company. | I've been at this company for 5 years. | Usar presente perfecto con "for" |

## Resumen

| Situación | Formal | Informal |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Saludar | Hello. / Good morning. | Hi! / Hey! |
| Presentarte | Hello, I'm [name], a [role] at [company]. | Hey, I'm [name]. I work with [tech]. |
| Conocer a alguien | Pleased to meet you. | Nice to meet you. / Good to meet you. |
| Despedirte | Have a good day. | See you tomorrow! / Later! |
| Agradecer | Thank you for your time. | Thanks! |
