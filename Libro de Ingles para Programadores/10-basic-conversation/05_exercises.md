## Ejercicio 7: Elige la respuesta apropiada

**1. Tu compañero dice: "Could you review my PR?"**
- a) I have many work.
- b) Sure, I'll take a look this afternoon.
- c) No, I don't want.

**2. Alguien nuevo se une al equipo:**
- a) Who are you?
- b) Welcome to the team! Let me know if you need anything.
- c) You are new.

**3. Estás atascado en un bug y quieres pedir ayuda:**
- a) Fix this for me.
- b) I have a doubt.
- c) I've been stuck on this for a while. Could you take a look?

**4. Tu compañero te explica algo que no entendías:**
- a) Thanks, that makes sense now!
- b) OK.
- c) I knew that.

**5. Quieres preguntar algo rápido por Slack:**
- a) Hi. (y esperar)
- b) Hey, quick question: Does the staging API support WebSockets?
- c) URGENT: Answer me now please.
